Introdução

Este guia foi criado para jogadores que desejam dominar o jogo, seja para alcançar o tempo mais rápido em uma speedrun ou completar tudo no modo 100%. Aqui você encontrará estratégias, rotas otimizadas e recursos usados por speedrunners e completistas ao redor do mundo.

As informações podem ser aplicadas a diversos jogos da franquia Lego Batman

Any% Speedrun guia

As rotas variam de acordo com a categoria e o jogo, mas geralmente envolvem:

    FDTs
    This game has Frame-Dependent-Tricks or FDTS that require high framerates to complete. This means that
    you must have a computer capable of high framerates to do these tricks. Lower refresh rates can help
    improve FDTs, but might not work for everyone. Test FDTs before committing to learning this run as they
    won’t work on every computer. FDTs are used in Any% and any category using story mode.

    General Knowledge
    Progress Saving
    In this game there are progress checkpoints that save your progress. Saving isn’t currently useful in
    this speedrun, but even when playing on continue without saving, you can store your progress and exit
    and reenter a level at a checkpoint. If you softlock and are forced to exit the level, you can still
    store progress to skip previously watched cutscenes and reenter the beginning of the room at the
    checkpoint.
    Location Panels
    In Lego Gotham City, there are location panels at main locations of the city. Once activated these
    panels will play a cutscene showcasing a villain character in Gotham City. After, activated panels can
    be teleported to using the map in the Batcave or through other activated panels. Some location panels
    must be activated by using laser vision or The Flash.
    Red Bricks
    Unfortunately most Red Bricks are locked during the story. This is because most Red Bricks must be
    unlocked as Lex Luthor, The Joker, or The Riddler which are unplayable in the hub during the story.
    Invincibility, Fall Rescue, and Super Build are all locked during the story.
    Vehicle Missions
    During all vehicle missions use 2 players to deal max damage against enemy vehicles. This isn’t really a
    skip or trick but should be used to save time.
    Hub Characters
    During the entire story you can only play as either Batman and Robin or as Batman and Superman in the
    hub except during one part. In between Harbouring a Criminal and Arkham Asylum Antics, there is a brief
    time where you can play as Bruce Wayne and Dick Grayson. You can unlock other characters during the
    story, but cannot play as them in the hub until you have completed the story.
    Hub FDTS
    Pause launches in the hub don’t work for Batman and Robin outside of the Batcave. For some reason in the
    hub, after completing a pause launch, Robin and Batman are forced back down in a gliding animation.
    However, pause flying still works for these characters.
    Suit Unlocking
    Batman and Robin suits are locked in the hub until they have been unlocked in the story.
    Clipping
    Clipping works better with v-sync on as you will not get launched or pushed back as easily when clipping
    with it on.

    Tricks (Not all used)
    Pause Fly - PF: https://youtu.be/l57JL0DD7_c
    FDT
    (V-sync off) Spam pause on a controller (not keyboard) while moving in a certain direction to “glide” in
    the air while losing little height. You can rapidly pause fly by spamming pause on controller and
    keyboard at the same time to lose even less height.
    Pause Launch - PL: https://youtu.be/ATeZqnbbL0Y
    FDT
    (V-sync 30-hz) Attack or jump roll on an upwards-sloped object, pause the game, and turn v-sync off.
    This is more precise but takes more time.
    (V-sync off) Attack or jump roll on an upwards-sloped object, Alt+Tab out and back into the game or
    Ctrl+Alt+Dlt followed by Esc, then immediately pause the game and unpause it. This takes less time but
    is less precise. Alt+Tab method can whitescreen or basically crash the game.
    Friend Grab Pause Launch: https://youtu.be/rWezHwh4IdI
    FDT
    Grab an invincible player on a slope, and complete the steps for a pause launch, and both players will
    launch into the air.
    Fly Fall Rescue - FFR: https://youtu.be/H5BDbM4xMn4
    (Using a flying character) Jump off a desired point to a fall plane, press special and follow it with an
    attack. This will make the character be rescued back to the original point.
    Fly Fall Rescue Cancel - FFR Cancel: https://youtu.be/nkEQadUxPfM
    (Using a flying character with heat vision) Perform an FFR and cancel it midway by holding attack to
    activate heat vision and stop the FFR. (Using FFR cancels to clip into things usually works better with
    v-sync on.)
    Camera Shield: https://youtu.be/ad-l3Id_MRc
    As invisible Sensor Suit Batman, shield a visible character from activating a camera by standing in
    between the camera and the visible character.
    Slow Fall Glide: https://youtu.be/qqr1a-Y6-LE
    As Bat Suit Batman you can fall slower while gliding by spamming jump instead of holding jump.
    Electricity Clip: https://youtu.be/NfSBa73yKMY
    Have a character die of electricity to clip through a wall. (Works better with v-sync on)
    AI Warp: https://youtu.be/GnrhRBEZqxQ
    Drop a player out and have the AI move a character to another area.
    Wall Jump: https://youtu.be/KauT9L9wg2s
    Spam jumps on certain walls to go up the wall.
    Trigger Clip: https://youtu.be/IXwl9Wm2VY0
    (V-sync on) When below a room trigger as a flying character, spam jump and special to clip through the
    trigger barrier and hit the trigger.
    Ground Pound: https://youtu.be/pXHFf9-tJjY
    Some characters can break things through walls or floors by pressing jump and attack near the breakable
    object.
    Friend Grab Clip: https://youtu.be/hxTh6-eX2pU
    Using the free extra Friend Grab, players can clip other players through some walls by pressing special.
    Hover Punch: https://youtu.be/gWClpgnII14
    As a flying character, when on a fall plane, spam punch to avoid being lifted up to the surface.
    Freeze Clip: https://youtu.be/leErxHz7G-I
    Freezing an environment behind a player, or freezing a player can be used to clip out of areas. (Works
    better with v-sync on)
    Camera Pan Skip - CPS: https://youtu.be/MazwMzr8eog, https://youtu.be/phFXklYzFXM
    With one player dropped in, rapidly switch characters or drop in player 2 to skip some camera pans.

    Play on continue without saving to remove time loss of the game saving.
    (Optional Recommendation) Use a double controller setup because PF does not work on keyboard and there
    are parts in the run where both players should PF at the same time for fastest strats.

    Softlocks/Crashes
    *Reminder, if you have hit a checkpoint and you softlock, then you can store progress and exit to come
    back into the level at the checkpoint.
    *Also, even if you haven’t gotten to a checkpoint, you will still skip beginning cutscenes when
    reentering a level.

    5-Chemical Crisis:
    Forklift Clip:
    If you do not clip through the ground while doing Forklift Clip, then you will be stuck in the caged
    area. Technically you are not actually softlocked, but will have to redo all the skips as Batman and
    face major timeloss.

    7-Unwelcome Guests:
    Silver Rocks:
    If you get stuck above or in the silver rocks while trying to hit the end trigger you are stuck and
    cannot complete the level.

    8-Destination Metropolis:
    End Room:
    If you miss the End Room by flying ahead or behind it as Superman, then you are stuck and cannot
    complete the level.
    9-Research and Development:
    Room 2 Flying:
    When flying as Superman OOB in R2, if you clip into any room area that is not the end area with the gold
    wall, then you cannot complete the level.
    Room 3 Start:
    In the start of room 3, sometimes you can crash while doing the x-ray puzzle as Superman. Usually v-sync
    on fixes this.

    11-Underground Retreat:
    Room 1 Batman PF:
    If you clip in the railroad in front of where the mech leg appears, then you cannot complete the level.

    12-The Next President:
    Joker Mech Slam:
    If Joker Mech slams Superman in place after Batman shines the second light onto the mech, then you
    cannot move as Superman.

    14-Tower Defiance:
    Tower Reentry:
    If Green Lantern clips into the tower in R1 after going OOB, and it is not in the first area or the
    trigger area, then you cannot complete the level.
    FFR Cancel:
    Using FFR Cancels can result in a crash sometimes when using heat vision OOB. So, avoid using heat
    vision OOB as much as possible.

100% (Completismo)

    Jogue os níveis 1-3 Com o Batsuit, deslize até Gotham Central (área Killer Croc)

    Desbloquear pontuação x2 (central elétrica)

    Desbloqueie o Localizador de Minikit (Gotham Park Lake)

    Com o Batsuit, deslize até Gotham South a partir do topo da Catedral de Gotham

    Desbloquear pontuação x4 (Delegacia)

    Desbloqueie o Detector de Tijolos Vermelhos (West Pier)

    Jogue os níveis 4-7 (desbloquear Superman)

    Clear Gotham (Norte, Centro e Sul)

    Nível de bônus de jogo (deve ter 175 tijolos de ouro agora)

    Jogue os níveis 8-15

    Desbloqueie os terminais Yacht Club e Ace Chemicals

    Desbloqueie Lex e Joker

    Desbloquear Stud Magnet, marcar x6, x8 e x10 e os tijolos vermelhos e dourados restantes

Dicas gerais:

  • Use um checklist ou mapa interativo para não esquecer itens escondidos.
  • Combine exploração com coleta eficiente de recursos.
  • Planeje a ordem das missões para reduzir viagens desnecessárias.

Dicas Avançadas

  • Assista a runs de recordistas no Speedrun.com para aprender técnicas e rotas atualizadas.
  • Treine segmentos específicos (splits) isoladamente antes de tentar uma run completa.
  • Grave suas tentativas e revise os erros para melhorar consistência.
  • Use ferramentas como LiveSplit para monitorar seu desempenho em tempo real.
